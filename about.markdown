The English for Work Alumni Network is a community of former Hopelink English for Work students.

<b>Our mission is clear: CONNECT - SHARE - SUPPORT - GROW</b>

<b>CONNECT</b> - The Alumni Network will enable members to develop professional and personal networking connections.

<b>SHARE</b> - The Alumni Network will curate real opportunities and experiences to share information, exchange knowledge, and increase access to career-related resources.

<b>SUPPORT</b> - The Alumni Network will create a safe space for members to feel supported and seen. No one is alone in their journey, everyone matters!

<b>GROW</b> - The Alumni Network will provide a platform for skill development, mentorship, and collaboration for individual and community growth.
