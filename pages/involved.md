<h1 class="pb-4">Get Involved</h1>
<h2>Help Alumni grow — in a way that works for you</h2>
<div class="container-md text-start mt-4">
<p>Alumni Community grows when members share their time, skills, ideas, and experience.</p>

<p>You do not need to take on a big role to help. You can support the community in small ways, lead one session, share your story, help with content, or apply for a Leadership Team role when you are ready.</p>

<p>Getting involved is an unpaid volunteer opportunity. It can help you practice English, build confidence, develop teamwork and leadership skills, support other Alumni members, and gain experience for your résumé, LinkedIn profile, or future interviews.</p>

<p>You are invited to help in a way that fits your life, time, and energy.</p>
</div>
