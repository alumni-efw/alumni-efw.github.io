<h1>About US</h1>
The English for Work Alumni community is for former Hopelink English for Work students.

<p class="fs-3"><b>Our mission is clear: CONNECT - SHARE - SUPPORT - GROW</b></p>

<b>CONNECT</b> - The Alumni Community will enable members to develop professional and personal networking connections.

<b>SHARE</b> - The Alumni Community will curate real opportunities and experiences to share information, exchange knowledge, and increase access to career-related resources.

<b>SUPPORT</b> - The Alumni Community will create a safe space for members to feel supported and seen. No one is alone in their journey, everyone matters!

<b>GROW</b> - The Alumni Community will provide a platform for skill development, mentorship, and collaboration for individual and community growth.
